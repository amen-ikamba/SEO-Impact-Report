Tech Developer Program Overview

DIFFICULTIES WITH EARLY CAREER HIRING

1

Resume Overload

Per Handshake's 2025 Internship Index, tech internships receive an average of 273 applications per posting, a 6.9% increase from last year. Some roles received upwards of 15,000 applications. AI tools and one-click apply has flooded roles with undifferentiated resumes.

2

Interview Pass-Through Rate at Historic Lows

Only 15% of applicants reach first-round interviews with a human recruiter (Veris Insights, 2025). Recruiting teams are getting smaller and selective with increasing reliance on AI automation or personal referrals that can be biased.

3

Onboarding

Computer Science grads often lack team-readiness and skills on modern software engineering practices. There is a steep learning curve slowing down onboarding, costing time and coaching bandwidth (SHRM + Glassdoor Insights).

4

US School-to-Work Crisis

Parents, schools, employers, and mentors all influence students' readiness, but few take full responsibility. Students must self-navigate the gap from classroom to career often without networks or relevant guidance (Shultz Family Foundation, 2025).

Our Solution: A Scalable Talent Accelerator

SE Tech Developer is a free, virtual immersion program that supports first and second-year college students majoring in Computer Science and Software Engineering. Through hands-on training, mentorship, and career preparation, students gain the technical and professional skills needed to succeed in the tech industry. The Tech Developer program bridges the gap between academic learning and workforce readiness, ensuring students are equipped to thrive in internships and early career roles.

SEO Tech Developer acts as a trusted navigator between organizations and students. We prepare, coach, and endorse students using an evidence-based approach so employers can trust their readiness and students don't fall through the cracks.

Strategic Recruitment

AI in Education

RESPONSIBLE & STRATEGIC AI INTEGRATION

Our Approach

How We Integrate AI Tools

  • Scaffolded Introduction: We gradually introduce AI tools throughout the curriculum, starting with foundational programming concepts before layering in AI assistance.
  • Guided Use: Students learn when and how to effectively use AI tools as learning aids, not replacements for understanding core concepts.
  • Critical Thinking Focus: Emphasis on evaluating AI-generated code, understanding its limitations, and developing problem-solving skills.
  • Industry Alignment: Training reflects real-world tech industry practices where AI tools are productivity enhancers, not substitutes for technical knowledge.
  • Balanced Approach: We ensure students build strong fundamentals first, then learn to leverage AI as a powerful tool in their development toolkit.

First Year Academy

GROWING THE PIPELINE FROM DAY ONE

First Year Academy is an early intervention for first-year college students who have declared or plan to declare a major in Computer Science. In this exploratory training program, 75+ participants learn and apply Python coding fundamentals to build their own project with Codio and Pygame. Participants who complete the First Year Academy will have mastered the core computer programming fundamentals required to successfully apply and gain admission into SEO Tech Developer following completion of their sophomore year.
